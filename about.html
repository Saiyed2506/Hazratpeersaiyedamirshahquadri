About Hazrat Peer Saiyed Amir Shah Quadri (R.A.)

Hazrat Peer Saiyed Amir Shah Quadri, also known as Mastan Bawa (R.A.), was a great Sufi saint from Ahmedabad, Gujarat. His dargah is a place of spiritual blessing, healing, and deep connection with the Divine.

📜 Date of Wiladat & Wisaal

He was born and passed away on the **25th of Rabi' al-Awwal**. This date is commemorated every year with love and devotion.

🌿 Spiritual Lineage

Hazrat Mastan Bawa (R.A.) belonged to both the **Hasani** and **Hussaini** bloodlines and traced his spiritual lineage through the noble family of **Shaykh Abdul Qadir Jilani (R.A.)**, particularly through his son **Shaykh Abdul Razzaq Gilani (R.A.)**.

🌟 Karamaat (Miracles)

Many stories of miraculous healings and spiritual guidance are attributed to Hazrat Mastan Bawa (R.A.). More details will be shared soon.

🕊️ Message & Legacy

Hazrat was known for his message of love, humility, spiritual service, and remembrance of Allah. He continues to guide hearts toward peace and unity through his teachings and presence.

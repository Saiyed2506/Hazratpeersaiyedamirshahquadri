📅 Annual Urs Celebration

The Urs of Hazrat Mastan Bawa (R.A.) is celebrated every year on the 24th and 25th of Rabi' al-Awwal. Devotees from all over gather to seek blessings, offer prayers, and participate in the spiritual ceremonies.

🗓️ Monthly & Weekly Gatherings

  • 25th Rabi’ al-Awwal: Special gathering (Mehfil-e-Sama) and Langar distribution
  • Every Friday: General Ziyarat & Du'a

🕌 Daily Ziyarat Timing

The Dargah is open every day for Ziyarat from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

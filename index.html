About Hazrat Peer Saiyed Amir Shah Quadri

Hazrat Peer Saiyed Amir Shah Quadri belongs to the noble lineage of Hasani and Hussaini, connected to the family of Shaykh Abdul Qader Gilani through his son Shaykh Abdul Razzaq Gilani. He is revered for his spirituality and karamat (miracles). Born and departed on 25th Rabiul Awwal, he is remembered for his teachings, compassion, and annual Urs celebrations held on the 24th and 25th of Rabiul Awwal every year.

Events & Gatherings

Annual Urs: 24th & 25th Rabiul Awwal
Monthly Gathering: 25th Rabiul Awwal - Langar and Mehfil-e-Sama
Daily Ziyarat Timings: 7 AM - 10 PM
